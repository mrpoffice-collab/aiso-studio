## The Opportunity: 60% of Websites Are AI-Invisible

### The Problem

By 2026, 50% of all searches will be AI-powered (Gartner). Yet 60% of websites can't be scraped by ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google SGE due to:

1. **robots.txt blocking** - Inadvertently blocks AI crawlers
2. **JavaScript rendering** - Content only loads client-side
3. **Bot detection systems** - CAPTCHA and aggressive anti-bot measures
4. **Rate limiting** - Blocks automated access
5. **Authentication walls** - Content behind login gates

**Business owners have NO IDEA this is happening.**

### The Solution

AISO Studio provides:
- **Unlimited AI searchability diagnostics** - Audit every business in your market
- **Exact problem identification** - Shows what's blocking AI crawlers

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked
✅ **Leading with free diagnostic** - 73% of prospects opened email when we attached a report
✅ **Live ChatGPT demo** - Showing them invisible in real-time closed 8/10 qualified calls
✅ **Vertical specialization** - Restaurants, real estate, and law firms had highest close rates
✅ **Recurring revenue focus** - Monthly monitoring created predictable income
✅ **Content upsells** - Once they trusted us with technical, they bought content too

### What Didn't Work
❌ **Generalist positioning** - "We do digital marketing" got ignored
❌ **Technical jargon** - Talking about "Googlebot vs GPTBot" confused prospects
❌ **Competing on price** - Cheap = not credible for technical work
❌ **Long proposals** - 20-page proposals had 5% close rate; 1-page had 35%
