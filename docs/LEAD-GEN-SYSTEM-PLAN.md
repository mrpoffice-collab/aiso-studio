## YOUR PERSONAL SALES WORKFLOW (Using Current Tools)

### For Persona B & C (Agency Outbound)

**Weekly Routine:**

#### Monday: Prospecting
1. Google "[city] marketing agency" or "[industry] SEO agency"
2. Run free audit on their site (as yourself)
3. If score < 70, add to your pipeline
4. Tag as "Persona B" or "Persona C" based on size

#### Tuesday-Wednesday: Outreach
1. Open Pipeline → Filter by "new" status
2. For each lead:
   - Generate proposal (captures pain points)
   - Send email using "Search Data Hook" template
   - Move to "contacted" status
3. Log activity in lead notes

#### Thursday: Follow-up
1. Filter Pipeline by "contacted" > 3 days ago
2. Send follow-up email (competitor comparison angle)
3. Move engaged leads to "engaged" status

#### Friday: Close
1. Check "engaged" and "demo_scheduled" leads
2. Prepare custom demo (their audit, their competitors)
3. Send proposal with pricing
4. Move to "negotiating" or "won"
